"The limit of my language is the limit of my world"

- Ludwig Wittgenstein

Explanation : The ability to name things, and understand each other when doing so, is one of the key things that sets us apart from the rest of the animal kingdom.The word limit does not stand for the extremity,rather it means the achievements of human to learn something which does not come within the reach of other animals.
